What is EnergyNest?

"Sharing Solar. Powering Communities."

The Problem We Solve

In India, millions of households with rooftop solar panels generate more electricity than they consume — especially during peak sunlight hours. This surplus energy is either wasted or sold back to the grid at very low rates (₹2–3/unit). Meanwhile, their neighbors pay ₹6–8/unit to the grid for the same electricity.

EnergyNest solves this by creating a neighborhood energy sharing network — solar households sell their surplus directly to nearby homes at fair peer-to-peer rates, everyone saves money, and zero clean energy goes to waste.

Zero Waste

Every kWh generated gets used within the neighborhood

Fair Pricing

Sellers earn more, buyers pay less than grid rates

AI Optimized

Smart matching, forecasting and distribution

How It Works (5 Simple Steps)

  1. Solar panels generate electricity: Households with rooftop solar panels generate electricity during daylight hours. A smart meter tracks real-time generation and consumption.
  2. AI detects surplus energy: Our AI engine continuously monitors every house. When generation exceeds consumption, it flags surplus energy as available for sharing.
  3. Smart matching with deficit households: The AI automatically matches surplus households with nearby homes that need power, optimizing for distance, price, and quantity.
  4. Energy flows through the local grid: Electricity is transferred through existing distribution infrastructure. No new wiring needed — just smart software on top of the current grid.
  5. Automatic billing and earnings: Transactions are recorded automatically. Sellers see earnings in their dashboard, buyers see savings vs grid rate — all transparent and real-time.
